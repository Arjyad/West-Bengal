West Bengal is a land of mountains, rivers; forests and the sea. The panoramic beauty of some tourist places in West Bengal is superbly eye-feast. Beautiful hill resorts, valleys, lakes, waterfalls and lovely natural scenery are scattered all over the State.

Cultural and Historical Heritage

There are places of historical and religious interest too to attract tourists. Bishnupur and Chandraketu Garh of Bankura, Rajardhibi of Burdwan, Santiniketan, Tarapith and Bakreswar of Birbhum and many other places like Gourh and Murshidabad can rightly boast of historical as well as religious associations. Kamarpukur, the birthplace of Sri Ramakrishna, Jayrambati, the birthplace of Sri Ma Saradamani and Radhanagar, the birthplace of Raja Rammohun Roy, are places of religious and historical importance. Mukutmanipur, Susunia Hill and several other tourist spots are modern additions that never fail to allure the sightseers.
